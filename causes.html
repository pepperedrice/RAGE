Our Values

By embracing these core values, R.A.G.E is focused on providing free education for children. Our Causes effectively guides our efforts and make a meaningful impact in the lives of children and communities.
Educational Access

RAGE believes that every child deserves access to quality education, regardless of their socio-economic background or circumstances. By providing free education programs, RAGE aims to break the cycle of poverty and empower children to reach their full potential.

Child Rights

RAGE advocates for the rights of children, including the right to education, protection from exploitation, and access to healthcare. Through awareness campaigns and advocacy efforts, RAGE seeks to raise awareness about child rights issues and promote policy changes that benefit children.

Community Development

RAGE recognizes the importance of community involvement in ensuring the success of education initiatives. By engaging with local communities, building partnerships, and fostering collaboration, RAGE works to create sustainable solutions that address the root causes of educational inequality.

Equity

Accountability

Holding ourselves accountable to our stakeholders, including donors, partners, and the communities we serve, by transparently reporting on our activities, outcomes, and impact.

Innovation

Embracing innovation and creativity in our approach to education, continually seeking new ways to improve and expand our programs and services.

Sustainability

Building programs and initiatives that are sustainable in the long term, ensuring that our impact endures beyond the initial intervention.
