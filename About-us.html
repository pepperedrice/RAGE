Welcome to R.A.G.E.

Welcome to R.A.G.E. - Rights and Aid Group for Education. Our mission is to provide free education to one million Nigerian children between the ages of 3 to 17 years. Join us in transforming lives and shaping a brighter future for the youth of Nigeria.

At R.A.G.E., we believe that education is the key to unlocking endless possibilities. Through our dedicated efforts, we aim to break barriers and ensure that every child has access to quality education, regardless of their background or circumstances.

Providing Nigerian Children with the tools for success through Education

Founded a decade ago by a group of passionate individuals with backgrounds in education, social work, and community development, RAGE was born out of the recognition of the profound impact that education can have on the lives of children and communities.

Witnessing firsthand the barriers faced by marginalized children in accessing quality education, the founders of RAGE were inspired to take action and make a difference.

Framing time

From its inception, R.A.G.E. has faced numerous challenges in its pursuit of providing free education to Nigerian children. Limited resources, lack of government support, and logistical hurdles have posed significant obstacles along the way. Additionally, cultural and societal barriers, such as gender inequality and child labor, have complicated efforts to reach and engage vulnerable children and families.

Despite these challenges, RAGE remains committed to its mission and continues to work tirelessly to overcome obstacles and create lasting change in the lives of Nigerian children.
