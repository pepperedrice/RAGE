Join Us

RAGE welcomes volunteers from diverse backgrounds who are passionate about making a difference in the lives of Nigerian children.
Volunteers can contribute their time, skills, and expertise in various ways, including:

  • Teaching & Tutoring
  • Outreach & Advocacy
  • Fund Raising & Logistics 
  • Administrative Support

Volunteers can help facilitate educational programs, assist teachers, and provide one-on-one support to students, leading enrichment activities, conducting workshops and seminars, mentoring programs, assisting with homework help centres, supporting students with learning English Language, facilitating parent engagement, etc. 

Volunteers can raise awareness about RAGE's mission, organize community events, and advocate for the rights of children, organize school visits and presentations, social media advocacy, partnership building, data collection and analysis and even accounting!

Volunteers can help organize fundraising campaigns, seek donations, and mobilize resources to support RAGE's programs and initiatives, event planning and execution, grant writing and research, corporate partnerships, inventory management, transportation, technical support, etc.

Volunteers can assist with administrative tasks, such as data entry, communication, and event planning, document management, communication, translation & interpretation, administrative assistance, accounting, volunteer coordination, etc. 

Lets Make a Difference!

At RAGE, we believe that education is the key to unlocking the potential of every child. Join us in our mission to empower one million Nigerian children through free education and create a brighter future for generations to come.
