Our Story

Rights and Aid Group for Education (R.A.G.E) is a non-profit organization dedicated to providing free education to underprivileged children in Nigeria. Located in Abuja, Nigeria, R.A.G.E is on a mission to transform the lives of one million Nigerian children through free education.

With a fiery passion for change, we believe that every child deserves the right to learn and grow, breaking barriers and unlocking their full potential. 

Join us in this epic journey as we pave the way for a brighter future, one child at a time.
